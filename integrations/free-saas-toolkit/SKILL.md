## Overview

Reference for free-tier SaaS tools across team collaboration, project management, analytics, forms, payments, and email marketing. Focused on tools with genuinely useful free plans (not just trials) for small teams and startups.

## Capabilities

- Recommend free collaboration and communication tools for teams
- Compare free project management platforms
- Evaluate free analytics solutions (privacy-friendly and feature-rich)
- Identify free form builders with reasonable limits
- Assess payment processors without monthly fees
- Compare free email marketing platforms by contact and send limits

## When to Use

- Setting up a new team's tool stack on zero budget
- Evaluating alternatives to paid SaaS subscriptions
- Building a startup's initial operational toolkit
- Recommending tools for community or open source projects
- Comparing free plans before committing to a vendor

## Process / Steps

1. **Identify team size** -- Free tier user limits vary widely (3 to unlimited).
2. **Check integration needs** -- Does the tool integrate with the rest of the stack (Slack, GitHub, etc.)?
3. **Evaluate export/migration** -- Can data be exported if outgrowing the free plan?
4. **Review feature gates** -- Which critical features are behind the paywall?
5. **Confirm permanence** -- Is this an always-free plan or a time-limited trial?

## Verification

- [ ] Free tier limits are sufficient for the team size
- [ ] Data export is available if migration is needed
- [ ] Critical features are not behind an expensive paywall
- [ ] The plan is always-free, not a time-limited trial
- [ ] Integrations with existing tools are confirmed
- [ ] Privacy and compliance requirements are met (GDPR, SOC2)
