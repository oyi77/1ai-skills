# Competitive Strategy

## Overview

Mindset skill for competitive strategy. Builds capability through deliberate practice and structured frameworks.

## When to Use

- Entering a new market or segment
- Responding to competitive threats
- Defining product positioning
- Investor diligence or board strategy
- **When NOT to use**: Early-stage pre-PMF (focus on customers, not competitors), or hyper-growth (move fast, don't react to every competitor move)


## When NOT to Use

- When the situation requires professional counseling or therapy
- For high-stakes decisions that need expert judgment
- When the skill conflicts with cultural or personal values


## Core Principles

- **Start small** — Begin with manageable competitive strategy practices and build incrementally
- **Be consistent** — Daily practice beats occasional intense effort
- **Track progress** — Measure improvement to maintain motivation
- **Reflect regularly** — Review what's working and adjust your approach

## Daily Practice

1. **Morning intention** — Set a specific competitive strategy goal for the day
2. **Active practice** — Apply competitive strategy techniques during real situations
3. **Evening reflection** — Review the day's competitive strategy moments and lessons learned
4. **Journal entry** — Record insights, wins, and areas for improvement

## Frameworks

- **OODA Loop** — Observe, Orient, Decide, Act — for rapid competitive strategy decision cycles
- **PDCA Cycle** — Plan, Do, Check, Act — for iterative competitive strategy improvement
- **After-Action Review** — What happened? Why? What to improve?
- **Deliberate Practice** — Focused effort on specific competitive strategy weaknesses

## Common Pitfalls

- **Perfectionism** — Waiting for ideal conditions instead of starting now
- **Inconsistency** — Practicing sporadically instead of building a routine
- **Isolation** — Not seeking feedback or accountability partners
- **Overwhelm** — Trying to improve everything at once instead of focusing

## Resources

- Keep a competitive strategy journal for tracking progress
- Find an accountability partner or mentor
- Set weekly competitive strategy challenges with measurable outcomes
- Review and adjust your approach monthly
