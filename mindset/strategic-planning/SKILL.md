# Strategic Planning

## Overview

Mindset skill for strategic planning. Builds capability through deliberate practice and structured frameworks.

## When to Use

- Setting annual or quarterly strategic direction
- Aligning teams on priorities
- Tracking execution and progress
- Adjusting strategy based on results
- **When NOT to use**: Tactical day-to-day execution (use sprint planning), or highly uncertain environments (use experiments instead)


## When NOT to Use

- When the situation requires professional counseling or therapy
- For high-stakes decisions that need expert judgment
- When the skill conflicts with cultural or personal values


## Core Principles

- **Start small** — Begin with manageable strategic planning practices and build incrementally
- **Be consistent** — Daily practice beats occasional intense effort
- **Track progress** — Measure improvement to maintain motivation
- **Reflect regularly** — Review what's working and adjust your approach

## Daily Practice

1. **Morning intention** — Set a specific strategic planning goal for the day
2. **Active practice** — Apply strategic planning techniques during real situations
3. **Evening reflection** — Review the day's strategic planning moments and lessons learned
4. **Journal entry** — Record insights, wins, and areas for improvement

## Frameworks

- **OODA Loop** — Observe, Orient, Decide, Act — for rapid strategic planning decision cycles
- **PDCA Cycle** — Plan, Do, Check, Act — for iterative strategic planning improvement
- **After-Action Review** — What happened? Why? What to improve?
- **Deliberate Practice** — Focused effort on specific strategic planning weaknesses

## Common Pitfalls

- **Perfectionism** — Waiting for ideal conditions instead of starting now
- **Inconsistency** — Practicing sporadically instead of building a routine
- **Isolation** — Not seeking feedback or accountability partners
- **Overwhelm** — Trying to improve everything at once instead of focusing

## Resources

- Keep a strategic planning journal for tracking progress
- Find an accountability partner or mentor
- Set weekly strategic planning challenges with measurable outcomes
- Review and adjust your approach monthly

```markdown
# Daily Practice Template

## Morning (5 min)
- Set intention for the day
- Review top 3 priorities

## During the day
- Practice the skill in real situations
- Note moments of success and struggle

## Evening (5 min)
- Reflect: What worked? What did not?
- Log one lesson learned
```
