## ❌ Challenge: Shopee Seller Emails

### Why This is Hard
- **Shopee doesn't publicly list seller emails** - they use internal chat system
- Most sellers don't have websites with public contact info
- Instagram/TikTok pages don't expose emails in HTML (JS-rendered)

### Tested Approaches
| Approach | Result | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Web scraping Shopee shop pages | ❌ No emails found | Shopee uses internal messaging |
| Searching Instagram/TikTok | ❌ JS-rendered content | Can't extract via web_fetch |
| Web search for brand + email | ❌ Only customer service found | Not seller-specific |

## 🎯 Strategy: Hybrid Approach

### Option 1: Manual Shopee Chat Outreach (Recommended)
**Workflow:**
1. Open Shopee shop: https://shopee.co.id/shop/homefix
2. Click "Chat to Seller"
3. Send this template:

```
Halo! Ini Veris dari BerkahKarya TikTok Content Agency.

Kami membuat video TikTok viral untuk produk Shopee. Kami sudah buat 5 sample videos yang bisa dilihat di: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1WZ5nVhee4nlAtvb83VmCEBRtaEAS8tv2

Untuk produk "Vinyl Floor Tiles (1 pack)" kalian, kami bisa buat 3 sample videos gratis.

Kalau tertarik, bisa kirim email address kalian via Shopee Chat ini, atau DM di Instagram @berkahkarya_official.

Terima kasih! 🙏
```

4. **Record in CRM**: Shop name, date contacted, response

### Option 2: Social Media DM
**Workflow:**
1. Search seller on Instagram/TikTok
2. Follow account
3. Send DM with:
   - Brief intro
   - Portfolio link
   - Offer for 3 free videos
4. **Record in CRM**

### Option 3: Create Landing Page (Best for Automation)
**Workflow:**
1. Create simple landing page: https://berkahkarya.co.id/tiktok-agency
2. Include:
   - Portfolio videos (embed from Drive)
   - Pricing tiers
   - Contact form
3. Share this link in Shopee Chat/Social DMs
4. Sellers fill form → Email automatically captured
