1. **Shopee Architecture**:

   - No public seller emails (by design)

2. **Outreach Strategy**:
   - Shopee Chat is primary channel
   - Social media DM is secondary
   - Email comes AFTER initial contact

3. **Content Generation**:
   - AI video generation takes 1-2 minutes per video
   - Quality is consistent with hyperrealistic settings
