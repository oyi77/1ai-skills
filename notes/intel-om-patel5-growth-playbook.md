## Revenue Growth Strategies (Section 7 — the money part)

### 1. Social Listening + Reply Engine
- Research agent scans reddit, twitter, linkedin, quora EVERY HOUR
- Find threads where people complain about competitors or ask for tool recommendations
- Writer agent drafts helpful replies that NATURALLY mention our product
- "This workflow alone has driven 450+ users for some founders with ZERO ad spend"

### 2. SEO + Blog Content
- Research agent finds exact questions target customers are googling
- Writer agent produces long-form blog posts optimized for those keywords
- One post per day. "Boring topics like 'how to fix [specific problem] in [your niche]'"
- "These posts compound over months and become your highest ROI channel"

### 3. Competitor Comparison Pages
- Pull competitor pricing, features, negative reviews from G2, Capterra, Trustpilot
- Create "[Your Product] vs [Competitor]" and "[Competitor] alternative" pages
- "These rank fast because people actively search for them before buying"
- Update monthly with fresh data

### 4. Short Form Content (ALREADY DOING THIS)
- Find viral posts and trending topics
- Repurpose into short form for TikTok, IG, LinkedIn
- Separate agent creates carousel/slideshow scripts from best blog posts
- Schedule 3-5 posts per day across platforms

### 5. Email Sequences
- Onboarding, feature announcements, re-engagement
- Monitor which emails get opened
- Rewrite underperforming emails automatically

### 6. Cold Outreach
- Find people who left NEGATIVE reviews of competitors
- Draft personalized DMs offering our product as solution
- "Never let the agent send directly from your account"
