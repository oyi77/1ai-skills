## Content Mix (Research-Backed)
- **60% Educational/Value**: Tips, facts, how-to → builds trust, gets saves
- **20% Engagement**: Polls, questions, tag-a-friend → algo boost
- **20% Promo/Direct sell**: Product links, urgency → revenue

## Engagement Optimization Tactics
1. Ask questions in captions (drives comments)
2. Use local language (Bahasa > English for ID audience)
3. Include CTAs (Comment, Tag, Share)
4. Educational content gets 3x more saves
5. Controversy/surprise hooks get more reach
6. Reply to every comment within 1 hour (algo signal)
7. Cross-promote between platforms
8. Use trending hashtags + niche hashtags combo
