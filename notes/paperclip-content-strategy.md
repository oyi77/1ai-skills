## Mission

Position Berkah Karya as the leading AI-native business partner for Indonesian and Southeast Asian entrepreneurs — producing content that educates, builds trust, and converts readers into clients and customers across all business verticals.

---

## Topic Pillars

### Pillar 1 — AI for Business Growth
*Primary audience: SME owners, startup founders, marketing managers*

Content that demystifies AI and shows concrete business applications.

- How AI tools transform marketing for small businesses
- AI automation case studies (real results, real numbers)
- "From manual to automated" step-by-step guides
- AI tools roundups and comparisons
- Indonesian/SEA business context: applying global AI trends locally

### Pillar 2 — Digital Marketing Mastery
*Primary audience: Marketing managers, business owners wanting to DIY*

Actionable, practical digital marketing education.

- SEO fundamentals and advanced tactics
- Social media strategy by platform
- Paid ads (Meta, Google, TikTok) — strategy and optimization
- Email marketing and automation
- Analytics and conversion rate optimization
- Content repurposing frameworks

### Pillar 3 — Future of Work & AI Talent
*Primary audience: Professionals, job seekers, HR teams, founders hiring talent*

Insights on AI's impact on careers and the talent market.

- Skills for the AI era
- Remote and async work best practices
- AI talent market trends (SEA focus)
- Career development in tech and marketing
- How companies are restructuring around AI agents

### Pillar 4 — Tech & Software for Non-Technical Founders
*Primary audience: Startup founders, product owners, business operators*

Practical technology guidance for building and scaling.

- No-code/low-code tools
- When to build vs. buy vs. AI-generate
- Software development with AI agents
- Product-market fit and MVP strategy
- Tech stack decisions for early-stage companies

### Pillar 5 — Berkah Karya Insider
*Primary audience: All audiences — builds trust and humanizes brand*

Behind-the-scenes content that builds brand affinity.

- Case studies and client success stories
- Our AI-native operations: how we run a company with AI agents
- Lessons learned building verticals (candid, not polished)
- Team introductions and agent spotlights
- Monthly metrics and transparency reports

## Target Audience Personas

### Persona 1: Budi — The SME Owner
- **Age:** 35–50
- **Context:** Runs a retail, F&B, or services business in Indonesia (1–30 employees)
- **Pain:** Wants digital presence but can't afford big agencies; overwhelmed by platforms
- **Goal:** More customers through digital channels, without hiring a full marketing team
- **Content that resonates:** Quick wins, case studies from similar businesses, practical tutorials

### Persona 2: Rina — The Startup Founder
- **Age:** 25–38
- **Context:** Building a B2B or B2C startup, raised seed or bootstrapped
- **Pain:** Needs marketing + sometimes tech help; budget constrained; time-poor
- **Goal:** Growth with limited resources; credibility in a crowded market
- **Content that resonates:** Playbooks, frameworks, "how we grew from X to Y" stories

### Persona 3: Dimas — The Corporate Marketing Manager
- **Age:** 28–40
- **Context:** Works at a mid-size company, leads a small marketing team
- **Pain:** Being asked to "do more with less" and "use AI" — but how?
- **Goal:** Adopt AI tools to scale output without growing headcount
- **Content that resonates:** Tool comparisons, productivity hacks, ROI-focused case studies

### Persona 4: Sari — The AI-Curious Professional
- **Age:** 22–35
- **Context:** Knowledge worker in any field — sensing AI will impact their career
- **Pain:** Anxiety about relevance; wants to upskill but doesn't know where to start
- **Goal:** Stay ahead of the curve, build marketable AI skills
- **Content that resonates:** "How to get started" guides, skill roadmaps, career pivots

## Content Quality Standards

- **Accuracy first:** No hallucinated statistics. All claims linked to sources.
- **Actionable always:** Every piece ends with a clear next step or takeaway.
- **Human review on all published content:** AI drafts, CMO approves.
- **Brand voice:** Direct, practical, optimistic, grounded. No buzzword fluff.
- **Bilingual preference:** Bahasa Indonesia for primary market; English for international.
