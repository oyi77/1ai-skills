## 🎯 What Makes People STOP Scrolling (Research-Backed)

### 1. HUMAN FACES (Top priority)
- Posts with human faces get **38% more likes** and **32% more comments** (Georgia Tech study)
- Authentic > stock photo — candid shots outperform posed 2:1
- Eye contact with camera = highest engagement
- **For our products:** Show person USING the product, not just the product

### 2. HIGH CONTRAST + BOLD COLORS
- High-contrast images get **74% more engagement** (Adobe research)
- Bright, saturated colors outperform muted/pastel on feed
- Dark backgrounds with bright text = highest readability on mobile
- **Color psychology:**
  - Red/Orange = urgency, limited time offers
  - Blue = trust, tech, professional
  - Green = money, growth, sustainability
  - Yellow = attention-grabbing, happiness

### 3. MINIMAL TEXT (20% rule)
- Facebook/Instagram penalize images with >20% text area
- Best performing: 0-20% text overlay
- If text: MAX 7 words, LARGE font, HIGH contrast
- Let the CAPTION do the talking, not the image

### 4. CLEAR FOCAL POINT
- One clear subject > cluttered composition
- Rule of thirds for subject placement
- Negative space draws eye to product/message
- Blurred background = professional look

### 5. EMOTIONAL TRIGGERS
- Before/After = highest engagement for product posts
- Problem → Solution visual storytelling
- Numbers/stats in image = 2x more shares (BuzzSumo)
- "How-to" step visuals get 3x more saves (Instagram data)

### 6. BRAND CONSISTENCY
- Consistent color palette across all posts
- Same font family (max 2 fonts)
- Recognizable template style
- Logo/watermark (small, bottom corner)

## 🔴 What NOT To Do (Anti-Patterns)

1. ❌ **Text-heavy images** — walls of text on bright backgrounds (what we had)
2. ❌ **Emoji in images** — renders as □ on many devices (our problem!)
3. ❌ **Generic stock photos** — decreases trust
4. ❌ **Pixelated/low-res** — instant scroll-past
5. ❌ **Random color per post** — no brand recognition
6. ❌ **Multiple messages in one image** — confuses viewer

## ✅ Optimal Image Formula for LYNK Products

### Template A: "Problem → Solution" (Educational 60%)
```
Layout: 1080x1350 (4:5 vertical)
Top 40%: Bold problem statement (large text, dark bg)
Middle 20%: Arrow/divider
Bottom 40%: Solution + product name + subtle CTA
Colors: Dark navy/black bg, white text, accent color per product
Font: Bold sans-serif (Montserrat/Inter)
Text: MAX 15 words total
```

### Template B: "Stat + Hook" (Engagement 20%)
```
Layout: 1080x1350 (4:5 vertical)
Top 30%: Big number/stat (e.g., "40%↑")
Middle 40%: One-line explanation
Bottom 30%: Product name + "Link di bio"
Colors: Gradient background (brand colors)
Font: Extra bold for number, regular for text
```

### Template C: "Product Showcase" (Promo 20%)
```
Layout: 1080x1350 (4:5 vertical)
Center: Product mockup/screenshot
Top: Product name + price tag
Bottom: Key benefit + CTA
Colors: Clean white bg, product as hero
Font: Product name in bold, benefit in regular
```
