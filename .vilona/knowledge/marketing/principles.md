# Vilona Marketing Knowledge Base

## Core Principles (Veris's Wisdom)

### 1. BerkahKarya Marketing Philosophy
- Data > Gut feeling
- Test small, scale winners
- Kill losers fast
- Attribution is everything

### 2. Channel Priorities (Crisis Mode)

### 3. TikTok Content Agency Offer

**Product:** AI-generated 9:16 videos (15 sec)
**Pricing:**
- Starter: IDR 3M/mo (20 videos = 150K/video)
- Growth: IDR 5M/mo (40 videos = 125K/video)
- Scale: IDR 8M/mo (80 videos = 100K/video)

**Unit Economics:**
- Cost: ~IDR 1K/video (AI generation)
- Margin: 100-150x
- Break-even: 1 client

**Target:** Shopee sellers with high-volume products

### 4. Meta Ads SOP

**Daily Checks:**
- [ ] Campaign P&L by ad set
- [ ] CTR > 1.5%, CPC trend
- [ ] Frequency < 3
- [ ] ROAS vs target

**Kill Triggers:**
- CTR < 1% after 1K impressions
- CPC up 30% from baseline
- Frequency > 5
- ROAS < 2 after 3 days

**Scale Triggers:**
- ROAS > 3 consistently
- CTR > 2%
- CAC improving

### 5. Creative Testing Framework

**The 3-3-3 Method:**
- 3 hooks
- 3 bodies
- 3 CTAs
= 27 combinations

**Spend per variant:**
- Test: IDR 300K (stat sig)
- Scale: IDR 1M+ (winners only)

### 6. Competitor Intelligence

**Daily Monitor:**
- 3 competitors' ad creatives
- Pricing changes
- Promotion timing
- Audience targeting shifts

**Tools:**
- Meta Ad Library
- TikTok Creative Center
- Google Ads Transparency

### 7. Crisis Mode Rules

1. Positive ROAS or kill
2. Scale only proven winners
3. Test budget max IDR 500K/day total
4. Every campaign must answer: Revenue when?
