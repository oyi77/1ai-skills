# Trading Team Orchestrator

Full trading team orchestrator for autonomous trading operations.

## Team Members

- **Researcher**: Market research and data collection
- **Strategist**: Strategy building and optimization
- **Risk Manager**: Risk assessment and position sizing
- **Executor**: Trade execution with guardrails

## Commands

### `run_autonomous`
Run autonomous trading cycle.

**Usage**: `run_autonomous symbol=XAUUSD mode=paper`

### `daily_briefing`
Get daily trading briefing.

**Usage**: `daily_briefing`

### `team_status`
Check team status.

**Usage**: `team_status`
