# AI Company Policies

## Transparency Policy

**Rule**: All AI-generated content must be labeled

**Requirements**:
- Email signatures indicate "Generated with AI assistance"
- Social posts include #AI or "AI-generated" tags
- Customer chats disclose AI involvement
- Internal docs can be unlabeled (internal only)

**Examples**:
```
This email was written with AI assistance.
Generated content - human reviewed
#AIGenerated
```

---

## Data Privacy Policy

**Rule**: No customer data shared with external AI

**Requirements**:
- Use local processing when possible
- Don't paste customer PII into AI prompts
- Anonymize data before AI analysis
- Store sensitive data locally only

**Allowed**:
- General business info
- Anonymized metrics
- Public company data

**Not Allowed**:
- Customer names/emails in prompts
- Personal identifiable information
- Financial account details

---

## Responsible AI Policy

**Rule**: AI can propose, humans decide

**Human Review Required For**:
- Contracts and legal agreements
- Payment processing
- Terminating employees/vendors
- Major financial commitments
- Customer data deletion requests

**AI Can Handle Autonomously**:
- Drafting initial content
- Research and analysis
- Routine customer responses
- Code reviews
- Project management

**Decision Framework**:
1. AI generates options
2. AI assesses pros/cons
3. AI makes recommendation
4. Human makes final decision
5. AI executes approved decision
