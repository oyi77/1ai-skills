# AI Workforce Structure

## Team: Revenue
**Goal**: Generate revenue through campaigns, deals, partnerships

**Skills**:
- marketing: Brand, campaigns, content
- sales: Deals, CRM, pipeline
- business-development: Partnerships
- content-creator: All content generation

**Workflow**:
1. marketing creates awareness
2. content-creator generates content
3. sales follows up on leads
4. business-development explores partnerships

---

## Team: Operations
**Goal**: Keep business running smoothly

**Skills**:
- project-management: Tasks, deadlines, sprints
- customer-support: Tickets, email, chat
- market-research: Intelligence, competitors

**Workflow**:
1. market-research gathers intelligence
2. project-management plans and executes
3. customer-support handles inquiries

---

## Team: Product
**Goal**: Build and ship products

**Skills**:
- subagent-driven-development: Implement features
- verification-before-completion: Test quality
- finishing-a-development-branch: Deploy, merge

**Workflow**:
1. subagent-driven-development implements
2. verification-before-completion tests
3. finishing-a-development-branch deploys

---

## Team: Governance
**Goal**: Maintain quality and compliance

**Skills**:
- receiving-code-review: Handle feedback
- agent-docs: Document everything
- systematic-debugging: Fix issues

**Workflow**:
1. receiving-code-review processes feedback
2. systematic-debugging identifies root cause
3. agent-docs documents decisions
