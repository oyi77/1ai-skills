**Learning:** [In Python, using `difflib.SequenceMatcher(...).ratio()` inside an O(n^2) nested loop creates a significant performance bottleneck due to its computational expense. However, by leveraging a mathematical upper-bound pre-check based on string lengths (`2.0 * min(len(a), len(b)) / (len(a) + len(b))`), we can entirely bypass the calculation when the theoretical maximum similarity falls below our target threshold.]
