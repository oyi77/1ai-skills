## Design Principles Extracted

### 1. Platform-First Approach
- **Primary:** Instagram (Square + Vertical)
- **Strategy:** Optimize for where ads convert best
- **Not prioritizing:** TikTok 9:16 format (unlike my previous assumption)

### 2. Dark Theme Preference
- High contrast with light text
- Modern, professional vibe
- Reduces visual noise
- Focus on message delivery

### 3. Minimalist Aesthetic
- Clean backgrounds (solid dark)
- No complex patterns/gradients
- Text as primary visual element
- Subtle color accents only

### 4. Format Versatility
- Same design adapted for multiple formats
- 1:1 for feed reach
- 4:5 for engagement/portrait
- Reusable content strategy

## Actionable Guidelines for JENDRALBOT

### Design Specifications

**Format Options:**
1. Square (1:1): 800x800 minimum for IG Feed
2. Vertical (4:5): 1024x1280 for IG Portrait/Story

**Colors:**
- Background: #000000 (pure black) or #101010 (near-black)
- Primary Text: #FFFFFF (white)
- Accent: Minimal use, consider:
  - #202040 (subtle blue) for trust
  - #200000 (subtle red) for urgency
  - #606080 (cool gray) for softer tone

**Typography:**
- Bold, high-contrast white text on dark background
- Clean, modern sans-serif styles
- Readable in mobile sizes (test at 320px width)

**Layout:**
- Top 20-30%: Hook/Headline (bold, large)
- Middle 40-50%: Body copy/subtext
- Bottom 20-30%: CTA/Price/Offer
