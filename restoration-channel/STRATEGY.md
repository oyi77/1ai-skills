# Restoration AI Channel Strategy

## Channel Concept
**Niche:** AI-generated satisfying restoration content
**Format:** YouTube Shorts (< 60s)
**Style:** Before/after transformations, ASMR-style

## Content Pipeline

### Option A: AI-Generated (100% Original)
1. Use GeminiGen.ai / Veo / Sora
2. Prompts for restoration videos:
   ```
   - "Rusty antique knife being restored to pristine condition, timelapse, satisfying"
   - "Old vintage camera restoration, cleaning process, before after, 4K"
   - "Abandoned rusty bicycle restoration, transformation timelapse"
   - "Vintage watch restoration, detailed cleaning, satisfying ASMR"
   ```
3. Add royalty-free music (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, YouTube Audio Library)
4. Upload as original content

### Option B: Compilation (Transformative)
1. Source 5+ different clips from:
   - Creative Commons videos
   - Licensed stock footage
   - Your own generated content
2. Create compilation: "Top 10 Satisfying Restorations"
3. Add:
   - Your voiceover/narration
   - Text overlays (rankings, descriptions)
   - Unique editing style
   - Royalty-free music
4. This is "transformative" = Fair Use protection

### Option C: React/Commentary
1. Show restoration clips
2. Add face cam or voiceover commentary
3. Educational angle: "How they restored this..."
4. Critique/rate the restorations
5. This is clearly transformative
