# Calendar Management

Advanced calendar management with intelligent scheduling, meeting automation, and cross-platform synchronization.

## Overview

This skill provides sophisticated calendar management capabilities including smart scheduling, meeting coordination, time zone handling, and integration with video conferencing platforms.

## Capabilities

### Smart Scheduling
- Find optimal meeting times
- Handle time zone conversions
- Avoid scheduling conflicts
- Respect working hours preferences

### Meeting Automation
- Auto-generate meeting agendas
- Send calendar invites
- Add video conference links
- Set up recurring meetings

### Coordination
- Poll attendees for availability
- Manage meeting rooms and resources
- Send reminders and notifications
- Handle RSVPs and responses

### Analytics
- Track meeting time by category
- Identify scheduling patterns
- Optimize calendar efficiency
- Generate time reports

## Usage Examples

### Schedule Team Meeting
```
Schedule a weekly team sync:
- Find time that works for all 5 team members
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Prefer Tuesday or Thursday afternoons
- Add Google Meet link
- Send invites with agenda
```

### Coordinate Cross-Timezone Meeting
```
Schedule a meeting with:
- Team in San Francisco (PST)
- Team in London (GMT)
- Team in Tokyo (JST)
Find a time that's during business hours for all locations
```

### Meeting Analytics
```
Generate a report for last month showing:
- Total meeting hours
- Meeting distribution by type
- Most frequent attendees
- Suggestions for optimization
```

## Best Practices

1. **Time Zones**: Always specify time zones explicitly
2. **Buffer Time**: Add buffer between back-to-back meetings
3. **Agendas**: Include clear agendas in invites
4. **Respect**: Honor "focus time" and "do not disturb" blocks
5. **Follow-up**: Send meeting notes and action items promptly
