## Hook Patterns

### Pattern 1: Old Way vs New Way
```
"Kamu masih [old_way]? Coba [new_way]..."
```
- **Example:** "Kamu masih edit video manual? Coba AI tools ini..."
- **Engagement Rate:** High (3-8%)
- **Best Format:** Tutorial / Demo
- **Best Niche:** AI Tools, Digital Marketing

### Pattern 2: Shocking Number
```
"[Number] [thing] yang [bisa] [bikin/bantu] kamu [outcome] dalam [timeframe]"
```
- **Example:** "5 AI tools yang bisa bantu kamu cuan Rp 5 juta dalam seminggu"
- **Engagement Rate:** Very High (5-12%)
- **Best Format:** Listicle
- **Best Niche:** Side Hustle, AI Tools

### Pattern 3: Confession / Personal Story
```
"Jujur, gue dulu [situation]. Sekarang [outcome]. Caranya?"
```
- **Example:** "Jujur, gue dulu gabisa masak. Sekarang omzet 30juta/bulan dari warung online."
- **Engagement Rate:** High (4-9%)
- **Best Format:** Storytelling
- **Best Niche:** Kuliner, Side Hustle

### Pattern 4: Warning / Controversy
```
"STOP [doing thing]. [Reason]. [Better alternative]."
```
- **Example:** "STOP jualan di marketplace sebelum kamu tau ini..."
- **Engagement Rate:** Very High (6-15%)
- **Best Format:** Controversy
- **Best Niche:** Digital Marketing, Side Hustle

### Pattern 5: Comparison / Before-After
```
"[Before state] → [After state] in [timeframe]"
```
- **Example:** "Omzet Rp 0 → Rp 10 juta dalam 30 hari pakai strategi ini"
- **Engagement Rate:** High (4-8%)
- **Best Format:** Before/After
- **Best Niche:** All niches

### Pattern 6: Question Hook
```
"Tau gak kamu bisa [outcome] cuma dengan [simple thing]?"
```
- **Example:** "Tau gak kamu bisa otomatisasi bisnis kamu cuma dengan 1 tools gratis?"
- **Engagement Rate:** Medium-High (3-7%)
- **Best Format:** Tutorial
- **Best Niche:** AI Tools, Digital Marketing

### Pattern 7: Secret Reveal
```
"Rahasia [expert/successful person] yang [gak banyak orang tau]"
```
- **Example:** "Rahasia content creator 1 juta followers yang gak banyak orang tau"
- **Engagement Rate:** High (4-10%)
- **Best Format:** Storytelling + Tutorial
- **Best Niche:** Education, Digital Marketing

### Pattern 8: Fear of Missing Out (FOMO)
```
"Kalau kamu gak [action] sekarang, kamu bakal [negative outcome]"
```
- **Example:** "Kalau kamu gak pake AI sekarang, bisnis kamu bakal ketinggalan 5 tahun"
- **Engagement Rate:** Very High (5-12%)
- **Best Format:** Educational + Warning
- **Best Niche:** AI Tools, Side Hustle
