# Viral Marketing System

## The GaryVee Content Machine

### Volume Strategy

**Daily Output:**
```
- 10-20 Instagram posts
- 5-10 TikToks
- 3-5 YouTube videos
- 20+ Tweets
- 2-3 LinkedIn posts
- 1-2 blog articles
= 100+ pieces daily
```

**Why Volume Works:**
- Algorithm favors frequency
- More chances to hit viral
- Builds audience faster
- Improves with repetition

### Content Pillars

**Jab, Jab, Jab, Right Hook:**
1. **Give value** (80% of content)
2. **Give value** (more education)
3. **Give value** (entertain or inspire)
4. **Ask for sale** (20% of content)

### Platform Strategy

**Native Content Per Platform:**

| Platform | Content Type | Frequency |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| TikTok | Short, raw, trend-jacking | 5-10/day |
| Instagram | Visual, lifestyle, quotes | 5/day |
| YouTube | Long-form, educational | 1-2/day |
| LinkedIn | Professional, business | 1-2/day |
| Twitter | Thoughts, threads, replies | 20+/day |

## The Hustle Framework

### Daily Execution

> "You're not working hard enough. Period."

**Minimum Viable Daily:**
- Wake up early (5-6am)
- Create before consuming
- Block time for content
- Engage with community
- Track metrics
- Iterate tomorrow

### Document, Don't Create

**Content Sources:**
- What you did today
- Conversations you had
- Books you're reading
- Problems you solved
- Lessons learned

**Don't overthink. Just document.**
