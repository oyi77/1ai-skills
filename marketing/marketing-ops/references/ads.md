## Campaign Planning

### Campaign Structure Framework

```
CAMPAIGN (Objective-level)
├── Ad Set 1 (Audience + Placement + Budget)
│   ├── Ad 1a (Creative Variant A)
│   ├── Ad 1b (Creative Variant B)
│   └── Ad 1c (Creative Variant C)
├── Ad Set 2 (Different Audience)
│   ├── Ad 2a
│   └── Ad 2b
└── Ad Set 3 (Retargeting)
    ├── Ad 3a
    └── Ad 3b
```

### Campaign Objective Selection Guide

| Business Goal | Meta Objective | Google Objective |
|--------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Brand awareness | Awareness | Display / YouTube |
| Website traffic | Traffic | Search / Display |
| Lead generation | Leads | Search / Lead Forms |
| App installs | App Promotion | App campaigns |
| E-commerce sales | Sales | Shopping / PMax |
| Video views | Engagement | YouTube |

### Audience Strategy Layers
1. **Cold** — Interest/behavior targeting, lookalikes (1-3%)
2. **Warm** — Website visitors, video viewers, social engagers (7-30 days)
3. **Hot** — Cart abandoners, email subscribers, past customers
4. **Exclusions** — Always exclude existing customers from acquisition campaigns

## Meta Ads

### Ad Format Specs

| Format | Image Size | Video Length | Primary Text | Headline |
|--------|-----------|-------------|-------------|----------|
| Feed image | 1080×1080 or 1200×628 | — | 125 chars (visible) | 40 chars |
| Feed video | 1080×1080 or 1080×1920 | 15-60s ideal | 125 chars | 40 chars |
| Stories/Reels | 1080×1920 | 15s stories / 30-60s reels | Overlaid | — |
| Carousel | 1080×1080 per card | Up to 240s | 125 chars | 40 per card |

### Creative Best Practices
- First 3 seconds must hook (text overlay, motion, face)
- Show the product/result in first 5 seconds
- Captions on all video (85% watch muted)
- UGC style outperforms polished for most DTC brands
- Test static vs video, square vs vertical
- Minimum 3-5 creative variants per ad set

### Audience Targeting Guide
- **Interest targeting:** Start broad (2-10M), let Meta optimize
- **Lookalike audiences:** Start with 1%, scale to 3-5% if stable
- **Custom audiences:** Website visitors (30-day), video viewers (50-75%), engagers (90-day)
- **Advantage+:** Consider for e-commerce with strong pixel data

### Meta Ad Copy Structure (3 variants minimum)

**Variant 1 — Problem/Solution (short)**
```
{Pain point question}
{1-line agitation}
{Product solves it — how}
{Social proof}
{CTA link}
```

**Variant 2 — Story/Testimonial**
```
"{Quote from customer about transformation}"
{2-3 lines elaborating on results}
{CTA}
```

**Variant 3 — Direct/Listicle**
```
{Bold benefit statement}
✅ {Benefit 1}
✅ {Benefit 2}
✅ {Benefit 3}
{Urgency/scarcity if applicable}
{CTA}
```

## Google Ads

### Search Campaign Structure
- 1 campaign per product/service category
- 5-20 keywords per ad group (tightly themed)
- 3+ Responsive Search Ad headlines, 2+ descriptions
- At least 1 keyword per match type (broad, phrase, exact) to test

### Responsive Search Ad Template
```
Headlines (up to 15, show 3):
H1: {Keyword} — {Value Prop}
H2: {Benefit or Feature}
H3: {CTA or Social Proof}
H4: {Free Trial / Discount / Offer}
H5: {Brand Name} — {Category}
...

Descriptions (up to 4, show 2):
D1: {Elaborate on value prop. Include keyword naturally. End with CTA.}
D2: {Social proof + differentiator. Why choose you over competitors.}
```

### Negative Keywords Strategy
Build negative keyword lists for:
- Competitor brand names (unless conquesting)
- Irrelevant modifiers: free, cheap, DIY, jobs, salary, definition
- Industry-specific: terms that match but have wrong intent
- Review regularly — search terms report weekly

### Performance Max Guidance
- Provide strong asset groups (images, videos, headlines, descriptions)
- Set clear conversion goals and values
- Provide audience signals (not targeting, just signals)
- Monitor placement reports — exclude low-quality placements
- Allow 2-4 weeks learning period before optimizing

## LinkedIn Ads

### Targeting Advantages
- Job title, function, seniority level
- Company name, size, industry
- Member skills, groups
- LinkedIn audience sizes tend to be smaller — expect higher CPCs ($5-15+)

### Recommended Formats
- **Sponsored Content** — Native feed posts, best for awareness/traffic
- **Message Ads** — InMail, best for high-value B2B offers (use sparingly)
- **Lead Gen Forms** — Pre-filled forms, lower friction than landing pages
- **Document Ads** — PDF carousels, good for thought leadership

## TikTok Ads

### Creative Requirements
- Vertical (9:16), 1080×1920
- 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 21-34 seconds)
- UGC style strongly outperforms branded content
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds (pattern interrupt)
- Captions/text overlays throughout
- Trending audio can boost performance

### TikTok Ad Script Template
```
[0-2s]  HOOK: "Stop scrolling if you {pain point}"
[2-8s]  PROBLEM: "I used to struggle with..."
[8-20s] SOLUTION: "Then I discovered {product}..."
[20-30s] PROOF: Show results, before/after, demo
[30-35s] CTA: "Link in bio" / "Click the button below"
```

## Budget Allocation

### Starting Budget Framework

| Stage | Monthly Budget | Allocation |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| Testing | $500-2,000 | 100% testing, no scaling |
| Early traction | $2,000-5,000 | 70% winning ads, 30% testing |
| Growth | $5,000-20,000 | 60% scaling, 20% testing, 20% retargeting |
| Scale | $20,000+ | 50% acquisition, 25% retargeting, 15% testing, 10% brand |

### Channel Budget Split (Default Starting Point)
- **E-commerce:** 50% Meta, 30% Google, 10% TikTok, 10% testing
- **B2B SaaS:** 40% Google Search, 30% LinkedIn, 20% Meta, 10% testing
- **Local business:** 50% Google (Local + Search), 30% Meta, 20% other
- **Creator/Personal brand:** 50% Meta, 30% TikTok, 20% other

Adjust based on results — move budget toward best ROAS channels.

## Performance Metrics

### Key Metrics by Objective

| Objective | Primary Metric | Target Range |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Awareness | CPM, Reach, Frequency | CPM $5-15 |
| Traffic | CPC, CTR | CTR > 1%, CPC < $2 |
| Leads | CPL, Lead Quality Score | Varies by industry |
| Sales | ROAS, CPA, AOV | ROAS > 3x for e-com |
| App Install | CPI, Day-1 Retention | CPI < $3 |

### Warning Signs (When to Pause/Change)
- CTR < 0.5% on Meta feed ads → Creative fatigue
- Frequency > 3 in 7 days → Audience too narrow or saturated
- CPA rising 30%+ week-over-week → Test new audiences/creative
- ROAS below break-even for 7+ days → Restructure or pause

## Ad Account Audit

### Audit Framework (50+ checkpoints)

**Structure**
- Campaign naming convention consistent?
- Proper campaign objective for each goal?
- Ad sets not overlapping audiences?
- Budget allocation matches priority?

**Targeting**
- Audiences sized appropriately (not too narrow)?
- Exclusions set (existing customers, converters)?
- Retargeting segments active and populated?
- Lookalikes based on best converters?

**Creative**
- Minimum 3 variants per ad set?
- Creative refreshed in last 30 days?
- Mix of formats (static, video, carousel)?
- Mobile-optimized (vertical, captions)?

**Tracking**
- Pixel/CAPI properly installed?
- Conversion events firing correctly?
- UTM parameters on all ad links?
- Attribution window appropriate?

**Waste**
- Ads with spend but zero conversions for 7+ days?
- Audiences with high CPA vs account average?
- Placements with high cost, low quality?
- Search terms (Google) with irrelevant traffic?

### Audit Output: Structured report with estimated monthly savings and revenue upside.
