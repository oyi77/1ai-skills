# Retention & Lifecycle Marketing Reference

Acquiring a customer costs 5-7× more than keeping one. For solo founders,
retention IS your growth strategy — every retained customer compounds revenue
without additional acquisition cost.

---

## The Retention Framework

### Customer Lifecycle Stages
```
ONBOARD → ACTIVATE → ENGAGE → RETAIN → EXPAND → ADVOCATE
  ↓          ↓          ↓        ↓         ↓          ↓
First      "Aha"     Regular   Renews/   Upgrades/  Refers
experience moment    usage     stays     buys more  others
```

### Key Metrics Per Stage
| Stage | Metric | Target |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Onboard | Onboarding completion rate | >80% |
| Activate | Time to first value (TTFV) | <24 hours |
| Engage | DAU/MAU ratio | >20% (healthy) |
| Retain | Monthly churn rate | <5% (SaaS), <10% (e-com) |
| Expand | Net Revenue Retention (NRR) | >100% (ideally >110%) |
| Advocate | NPS score | >40 |

## Onboarding (First 7 Days)

### The 3 Onboarding Principles
1. **Get them to value FAST** — remove every step between signup and "aha moment"
2. **Show, don't tell** — interactive walkthroughs > documentation dumps
3. **Celebrate progress** — every completed step should feel like achievement

### Onboarding Email Sequence
```
Day 0 (Immediate): Welcome + single most important first action
Day 1: Quick-start guide or video tutorial
Day 3: "Did you try {key feature}?" + how it helps
Day 5: Customer success story (someone like them)
Day 7: Check-in — "Need help with anything?"
Day 14: Power user tip (advanced feature)
Day 21: Value recap — show them what they've achieved
```

### Activation Checklist
Define 3-5 "activation events" that predict retention:
```
Example (SaaS):
  □ Created first project
  □ Invited a team member
  □ Completed first workflow
  □ Connected an integration
  □ Used the product 3 days in a row

Track: Users who complete 3+ activation events within 7 days
retain at 3× the rate of users who complete 0-1.
```

## Churn Prevention

### Churn Signals (Monitor These)
| Signal | Risk Level | Action |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| No login in 7 days | Medium | Automated re-engagement email |
| No login in 14 days | High | Personal check-in (WhatsApp/email) |
| No login in 30 days | Critical | Offer help or incentive to return |
| Support ticket unresolved >48h | High | Escalate immediately |
| Downgrade request | High | Call to understand, offer alternative |
| Negative feedback/review | High | Respond within 4 hours |
| Usage dropped 50%+ MoM | Medium | Trigger re-engagement workflow |
| Credit card expiring | Medium | Send update reminder 14 days before |
| Competitor mentioned in support | High | Understand why, address gaps |

### Churn Prevention Workflows

**At-Risk User (No login 7 days):**
```
Day 7:  "We miss you" email — highlight what's new + value reminder
Day 10: Push notification / WhatsApp — "Your {data/project} is waiting"
Day 14: Personal email from founder — "Is everything okay?"
Day 21: Offer: extended trial, free consultation, or discount
Day 30: Final: "Your account will be paused. Here's how to reactivate."
```

**Downgrade/Cancel Request:**
```
1. Ask WHY (short survey: too expensive, missing feature, switched, not using)
2. If price → Offer annual discount or smaller plan
3. If feature → Add to roadmap, share timeline, offer workaround
4. If competitor → Ask what they offer that you don't (competitive intel!)
5. If not using → Offer re-onboarding call
6. If still canceling → Make it easy (don't trap them), ask for feedback
7. After cancel → Win-back email in 30/60/90 days
```

## Expansion Revenue

### Upsell/Cross-sell Strategy
| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Hitting plan limits | "You're at 90% of your limit. Upgrade to get..." |
| Using product daily | "Power users love {premium feature}. Try it free for 7 days." |
| Achieving results | "You've saved 50 hours! Imagine what {advanced feature} could do." |
| Team member invited | "Unlock team features with our Team plan." |
| Anniversary | "Happy 1 year! Here's 20% off annual upgrade as thanks." |

### Expansion Email
```
Subject: You've outgrown your current plan (that's a good thing!)

Hey {name},

Quick heads up — you've {specific usage milestone}.

That means {product} is working for you, which is awesome.

To keep growing, you might want to check out {next tier},
which gives you {2-3 specific benefits relevant to their usage}.

{Existing customer} upgraded last month and {specific result}.

Want to see how it works? {CTA: upgrade link or demo link}

{Your name}

PS: Reply to this email if you have questions — I read every one.
```

## Customer Feedback System

### NPS (Net Promoter Score)
Send at Day 30, Day 90, then quarterly:
```
"On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend {product} to a colleague?"

0-6 (Detractors): Follow up within 24h. Ask what to improve.
7-8 (Passives): Send thank you. Ask what would make it a 10.
9-10 (Promoters): Ask for review/testimonial. Offer referral incentive.
```

### Review Collection System
```
Trigger: Customer achieves a milestone or gives positive feedback

Automated message:
"Hey {name}! So glad to hear {specific positive thing}.

Would you mind sharing your experience? A quick review helps
others like you find us:

→ {Google review link}
→ {G2/Capterra link if B2B}
→ {Tokopedia/Shopee review if marketplace}

Even 2-3 sentences help enormously. Thank you! 🙏"
```

### Testimonial Request Template
```
Hi {name},

You mentioned {specific positive result}. That's amazing!

Would you be open to sharing a quick testimonial?
It can be as simple as answering these 3 questions:

1. What was the problem before {product}?
2. How did {product} help?
3. What specific result did you get?

Can be text, voice memo, or even a 30-second video.
I'll handle the editing — you just share your honest experience.

{Your name}
```

## Loyalty & Referral Programs

### Simple Referral Program (Solo-Founder Friendly)
```
HOW IT WORKS:
1. Customer shares unique referral link
2. Friend signs up using the link
3. Both get a reward

REWARD OPTIONS:
- Credit: "Give $10, Get $10"
- Feature: "Refer a friend, unlock premium feature for a month"
- Subscription: "Refer 3 friends, get a free month"
- Tiered: 1 referral = small reward, 5 = bigger, 10 = VIP status

TRACKING:
- Use Dub (link attribution) or built-in referral code system
- Each customer gets: yourdomain.com/ref/{their-code}
- Track: referrals sent, clicked, converted, revenue attributed
```

### Making Referrals Easy
- One-click sharing (WhatsApp, email, social pre-populated)
- QR code on packaging or receipts
- In-app "Share" button at moments of delight
- Post-purchase page: "Know someone who'd love this?"
- NPS 9-10 response: automatic referral ask

## Win-Back Campaigns

### Win-Back Email Sequence (After Cancellation)
```
Day 30:  "We've been improving" — share 2-3 new features/fixes
Day 60:  "Here's what you're missing" — customer success story
Day 90:  Special offer — "Come back and get {incentive}"
Day 180: Final touch — "We'd love to have you back. Here's {best offer}."
```

### Win-Back Message Formula
```
ACKNOWLEDGE: "We know you moved on, and we respect that."
SHOW CHANGE: "Since you left, we've {specific improvements}."
SOCIAL PROOF: "{X} customers came back and found {result}."
INCENTIVE: "If you want to give us another try: {offer}."
EASY: "One click to reactivate: {link}"
```

## Solo Founder Retention Automation Stack

### Minimum Viable Retention System
```
Tool: Your email platform (Brevo, ConvertKit, etc.) + WhatsApp

Automated workflows:
1. Onboarding sequence (Day 0-21) → ACTIVATE
2. Usage milestone emails → ENGAGE
3. Inactivity trigger (7/14/30 day) → RETAIN
4. NPS survey (Day 30, quarterly) → FEEDBACK
5. Review/referral request → ADVOCATE
6. Upsell trigger (usage limits, anniversaries) → EXPAND
7. Win-back sequence (post-cancel) → RECOVER

Total setup time: ~4 hours
Revenue impact: 20-40% reduction in churn = equivalent to doubling acquisition
```
