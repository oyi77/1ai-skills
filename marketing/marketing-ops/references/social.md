# Social Media Mode Reference

## Content Calendar Framework

### Monthly Calendar Template
```
Week 1: {Theme: Educational}
  Mon: Blog post promotion (LinkedIn, Twitter)
  Tue: Quick tip / how-to (Instagram, TikTok)
  Wed: Industry news commentary (Twitter, LinkedIn)
  Thu: Behind-the-scenes / team (Instagram Stories)
  Fri: Engagement post — poll or question (All platforms)

Week 2: {Theme: Social Proof}
  Mon: Customer testimonial (LinkedIn, Facebook)
  Tue: Case study highlights (Twitter thread, LinkedIn)
  Wed: Product demo / feature (TikTok, Instagram Reels)
  Thu: User-generated content reshare (Instagram, Twitter)
  Fri: Community spotlight (All platforms)

Week 3: {Theme: Thought Leadership}
  Mon: Data/insight post (LinkedIn, Twitter)
  Tue: Myth-busting or hot take (Twitter, TikTok)
  Wed: Tutorial or walkthrough (YouTube, Instagram)
  Thu: Industry trend analysis (LinkedIn)
  Fri: Fun / relatable content (Instagram, TikTok)

Week 4: {Theme: Promotional}
  Mon: Product benefit highlight (All platforms)
  Tue: FAQ answered (Instagram Stories, TikTok)
  Wed: Comparison / vs. post (LinkedIn, Twitter)
  Thu: Limited offer or event promo (All platforms)
  Fri: Weekly recap + CTA (Email + Social)
```

### Engagement & Community Management
- Respond to comments within 2 hours during business hours
- Like/react to every comment on your posts
- Ask follow-up questions to drive conversation
- Reshare and credit UGC with permission
- Join relevant conversations in your niche (don't just broadcast)
- Track brand mentions with alerts

### Hashtag Strategy
- Research 30 hashtags per content pillar
- Mix sizes: 10 large (500K+), 10 medium (10K-500K), 10 niche (<10K)
- Rotate sets to avoid being flagged as spam
- Track which hashtags drive the most reach
- Create 1 branded hashtag for your community

## Social Media Metrics & Benchmarks

### Key Metrics by Platform

| Metric | LinkedIn | Instagram | Twitter/X | TikTok |
|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Good engagement rate | >2% | >3% | >1% | >5% |
| Good follower growth | >3%/mo | >2%/mo | >2%/mo | >5%/mo |
| Avg organic reach | 5-10% | 10-20% | 2-5% | 15-30% |
| Best post types | Carousels, Stories | Reels, Carousels | Threads, Images | Duets, Trends |

### Content Performance Tracking
For each post, track:
- **Reach** — how many unique people saw it
- **Engagement** — likes + comments + shares + saves
- **Engagement rate** — engagement ÷ reach (or followers)
- **Link clicks** — if CTA included
- **Saves/bookmarks** — strongest signal of valuable content
- **Shares** — strongest signal of viral potential

## Video Content Strategy

### Short-Form Video (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)

**Hook patterns that stop the scroll (first 1-3 seconds):**
- Text overlay with bold claim + visual disruption
- "Stop scrolling if you {pain point}"
- "Here's what nobody tells you about {topic}"
- "I tested {thing} for {time period}. Here's what happened."
- Start mid-action (no slow intros)

**Structure for educational short-form:**
```
[0-3s]   HOOK: Bold statement or question
[3-15s]  SETUP: Quick context (who, what, why)
[15-45s] VALUE: The actual tip/insight/demonstration
[45-55s] PROOF: Result, data, or social proof
[55-60s] CTA: Follow, save, comment, link in bio
```

**Structure for promotional short-form:**
```
[0-2s]   HOOK: Problem or desire
[2-10s]  DEMO: Show the product in action
[10-25s] BENEFIT: What it does for them
[25-30s] CTA: Where to get it
```

### Long-Form Video (YouTube)
- Title: keyword-rich, curiosity-driven, <70 chars visible
- Thumbnail: face + emotion + bold text + high contrast
- First 30 seconds: hook + preview what they'll learn
- Chapters: add timestamps for key sections
- End screen: subscribe CTA + related video
- Description: keyword-rich summary + links + timestamps
- Tags: 5-10 relevant tags including variations

### Video Repurposing Workflow
```
Long-form video (YouTube, 10-15 min)
  → Extract 3-5 best clips (30-60s each) → Reels/TikTok/Shorts
  → Transcribe → Blog post
  → Pull key quotes → Social graphics
  → Audio extract → Podcast episode
  → Slides/visuals → LinkedIn carousel
```
