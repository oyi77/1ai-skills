# Partnerships & Affiliate Programs Reference

Partner-sourced deals close at 2-3x the rate of cold outbound.
SaaS affiliate programs drive 10-22% of MRR.
This is the highest-leverage channel most solo founders ignore.

---

## Partnership Progression (By MRR Stage)

```
$0 MRR       → Co-marketing (free, relationship-based)
$1-5K MRR    → Referral partnerships (commission-based)
$5-10K MRR   → Integration partnerships (build into ecosystems)
$10K+ MRR    → Formal affiliate program (scaled distribution)
$50K+ MRR    → Strategic partnerships (co-sell, reseller)
```

## Co-Marketing Partnerships ($0 Cost)

### What Is Co-Marketing
Two non-competing companies with overlapping audiences create
content together. Both get exposure to each other's audience.

### Co-Marketing Tactics
| Tactic | Effort | Reach |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Guest blog post swap | Low | Medium |
| Joint webinar | Medium | High |
| Podcast interview swap | Low | Medium |
| Social media cross-promotion | Low | Low-Medium |
| Joint ebook/report | High | High |
| Bundle deal (both products discounted) | Medium | High |
| Newsletter mention swap | Low | Medium |

### Finding Co-Marketing Partners
Look for companies that:
- Serve the same ICP but sell a different product
- Have a similar audience size (±50% of yours)
- Produce content regularly (active blog, newsletter, social)
- Are not competitors (complementary, not competing)

**Outreach template:**
```
Subject: Collab idea — {Your brand} × {Their brand}

Hi {Name},

I run {your product} — we help {ICP} with {problem}.
I've been following {their company}'s content about {topic} and love it.

Since we both serve {shared audience} but with different products,
I think there's a natural collaboration opportunity:

{Specific idea: guest post swap, joint webinar, bundle deal}

Would you be open to chatting about it?

{Your name}
```

## Referral Partnerships

### Structure
```
Partner refers customer → Customer signs up → Partner gets commission
                                             (or credit, or feature access)
```

### Commission Models
| Model | Structure | Best For |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| Revenue share | 20-30% recurring | SaaS with recurring revenue |
| Flat fee | $50-200 per referral | One-time purchases |
| Credit | Account credit for both | Freemium/low-price products |
| Tiered | Higher % at higher volume | Scaling partnerships |

### Standard SaaS Affiliate Terms
- Commission: 20-30% recurring (lifetime or 12 months)
- Cookie window: 60-90 days
- Payout: Monthly via PayPal/Stripe/Wire
- Minimum payout: $50-100
- Attribution: last-click with unique referral link

## Affiliate Program Setup

### When to Launch
- You have at least 50 paying customers (proves product-market fit)
- Your product has clear, demonstrable value (easy to recommend)
- You can handle increased onboarding volume
- You have tracking infrastructure (referral links, attribution)

### Affiliate Program Components
```
1. LANDING PAGE: Explain the program, commission, how to join
2. SIGNUP FORM: Name, email, website/audience, promotion plan
3. DASHBOARD: Referral links, click tracking, earnings, payouts
4. CREATIVE: Banners, email templates, social posts, swipe copy
5. TRACKING: Unique links per affiliate, cookie-based attribution
6. PAYOUTS: Automated monthly payments above minimum threshold
```

### Tools for Solo Founders
| Tool | Cost | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| Dub Partners | Free tier available | Link tracking + affiliate |
| Tolt | ~$29/mo (0% transaction) | SaaS affiliate programs |
| Rewardful | $49/mo | Stripe-integrated SaaS |
| FirstPromoter | $49/mo | Full affiliate management |
| Manual (spreadsheet) | Free | Testing with <10 affiliates |

### Recruiting Affiliates
1. **Existing customers** — happiest users are best promoters
2. **Content creators** — bloggers, YouTubers in your niche
3. **Complementary tools** — products your users also use
4. **Communities** — active members in your space
5. **Review sites** — G2, Capterra reviewers who rated you well

### Affiliate Enablement Kit
Provide affiliates with:
```
□ Unique referral link (branded if possible)
□ Commission structure and payment terms
□ Product overview (features, pricing, differentiators)
□ Comparison charts (vs competitors)
□ Email swipe copy (3 versions)
□ Social media posts (5+ ready-to-post)
□ Banner ads (3 sizes)
□ Case study / testimonial they can share
□ FAQ document (for their audience's questions)
□ Demo video link
```

## Integration Partnerships

### Building Into Other Ecosystems

| Ecosystem | How to Partner | Benefit |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Zapier | Build a Zap integration | Discovery in Zapier marketplace |
| Make (Integromat) | Build a module | International audience |
| n8n | Build a node | Developer audience |
| Slack | Build a Slack app | Enterprise discovery |
| Shopify | Build a Shopify app | E-commerce audience |
| WordPress | Build a plugin | Massive install base |
| Chrome Web Store | Build an extension | Easy distribution |
| VS Code | Build an extension | Developer audience |

### Integration as Marketing
Each integration creates:
- A marketplace listing (discoverability)
- A backlink from the partner's site (SEO)
- A co-marketing opportunity (joint announcement)
- An {Your Product} + {Partner} SEO page (programmatic SEO)
- A switching cost (harder to leave once integrated)

## AppSumo Launch Strategy

### What AppSumo Is
- Marketplace of 1.4M+ tech-savvy buyers
- Sells lifetime deals (LTD) for SaaS products
- You get: cash upfront + user base + feedback
- They take: 50-70% commission

### When AppSumo Makes Sense
✅ Early stage, need cash and feedback fast
✅ Product is stable but needs user validation
✅ You can handle a surge of support requests
✅ You want to build social proof quickly

❌ Product-market fit isn't proven yet
❌ You can't handle 500+ new users quickly
❌ Your pricing is already working well
❌ LTD users will overload your infrastructure

### AppSumo Success Metrics
- Typical: $10K-50K in first month
- Top performers: $100K+ in launch period
- Convert 5-10% of LTD users to annual subscribers later
- Use feedback from LTD users to improve product rapidly

## Partnership Measurement

### Track Per Partner
| Metric | What to Measure |
|--------|----------------|
| Referral volume | Clicks + signups per partner |
| Conversion rate | Signups → Paid per partner |
| Revenue attributed | Total revenue from partner referrals |
| Commission paid | What you paid out |
| ROI | Revenue attributed ÷ Commission paid |
| Partner satisfaction | NPS or feedback from partners |

### Healthy Partnership Benchmarks
- Partner-sourced revenue: 10-25% of total revenue
- Partner conversion rate: 2-3x higher than cold traffic
- Average 5-10 active affiliates driving meaningful volume
- Commission payout: 15-25% of partner-sourced revenue
