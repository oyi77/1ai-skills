## Shopee Optimization

### Product Listing SEO Formula
```
Title format: [Brand] + [Product Name] + [Type/Model] + [Key Specs] + [Benefit]

Example:
❌ "Kaos Keren"
✅ "Kaos Pria Oversize Cotton Combed 30s Premium Adem Tidak Luntur"
```

**Title rules:**
- Max 120 characters (mobile-optimized)
- Front-load most searchable keywords
- Include material, size range, key benefit
- Don't use special characters or all caps
- Include Indonesian AND English keywords if applicable

### Shopee Fee Calculator (2026)
```
Total seller cost per transaction:
  Admin fee:           6-10% (Category A = 10%)
  Service fee:         2.8%
  Payment fee:         1.5-2%
  Shipping subsidy:    varies
  ─────────────────
  Total deduction:     ~15-28% per sale

Pricing formula:
  Selling price = (Product cost + packaging + desired profit) ÷ (1 - total fee %)

Example:
  Product cost: Rp 50,000
  Packaging: Rp 5,000
  Desired profit: Rp 30,000
  Total fees: 20%
  Selling price = Rp 85,000 ÷ 0.80 = Rp 106,250 → Round to Rp 109,000
```

### Shopee Ranking Factors
1. **Sales velocity** — recent orders/week (most important)
2. **Conversion rate** — views to orders ratio
3. **Product rating** — 4.5+ stars
4. **Response rate** — reply to chat within 12 hours
5. **Shipping speed** — ship within 1 day
6. **Keyword relevance** — title + description match search
7. **Shopee ads** — paid boost increases visibility

### Shopee Growth Tactics
- Join Flash Sale and Shopee campaigns (massive traffic boost)
- Use Shopee Live for product demos (3x conversion vs static listing)
- Set up voucher bundle: "Beli 2 Gratis 1" (Buy 2 Get 1 Free)
- Enable Gratis Ongkir (free shipping) — conversion increases 20-40%
- Use Star Seller badge requirements as optimization checklist
- Cross-list top products to TikTok Shop for multi-platform coverage

## TikTok Shop Optimization

### Why TikTok Shop Dominates Indonesia
- Lowest seller fees: 1% + Rp 20,000 per transaction
- Live selling drives 3x higher conversion than static listings
- 125M+ monthly active users in Indonesia
- Content-to-commerce pipeline (viral video → instant purchase)

### TikTok Shop Success Formula
```
CONTENT (entertaining) + PRODUCT (visible) + CTA (seamless) = Sales
```

### Live Selling Best Practices
- Stream 2-4 hours minimum (algorithm rewards duration)
- Peak hours: 11AM-1PM, 7PM-10PM (Indonesia time)
- Hook viewers in first 5 seconds (energy, promotion, demo)
- Show products live with close-up demonstrations
- Create urgency: "Stok tinggal 5!" (Only 5 left!)
- Pin product link during demo
- Interact with comments by name
- Use co-host format if possible (keeps energy up)

### TikTok Shop Content Strategy
- Post 2-3 product videos daily (15-30 seconds)
- Hook formula: Problem → Show Product → Result → Price → CTA
- Use trending sounds (increases distribution)
- Before/after format works for most products
- Unboxing and "review jujur" (honest review) style builds trust
- Repost best-performing content to Instagram Reels

## Indonesian Cultural Marketing Calendar

### Peak Marketing Seasons

| Period | Event | Marketing Opportunity |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| Jan-Feb | New Year, Back to School | Education products, planning tools |
| Mar-Apr | **Ramadan** (varies) | BIGGEST spending season (+20-30%) |
| Apr-May | **Lebaran/Eid** | THR spending, gifts, fashion, travel |
| Jun-Jul | Mid-year sale, school holidays | Travel, electronics, family activities |
| Aug | Independence Day (17 Agustus) | Patriotic campaigns, national pride |
| Sep-Oct | Seasonal promotions | Electronics, back-to-school |
| Nov | **11.11 Sale** | Flash sales, massive discounts |
| Dec | **12.12 Sale**, Christmas, New Year | Year-end shopping spree |

### Ramadan Marketing Guide

**Phase 1: Pre-Ramadan (2 weeks before)**
- Teaser campaigns: "Persiapan Ramadan"
- Stock up promotions, early-bird deals
- Content: preparation tips, product recommendations

**Phase 2: Early Ramadan (Week 1-2)**
- Focus: spiritual content, family values, charitable giving
- Tone: warm, respectful, community-focused
- Products: food, prayer items, home decoration
- Digital engagement spikes during Sahur (3-5 AM) and after Iftar (7-10 PM)

**Phase 3: Mid-Ramadan (Week 2-3)**
- Consumers start THR (holiday bonus) shopping
- Fashion, gifts, and electronics surge
- Content: "Ide Hampers Lebaran" (gift box ideas)
- Start Mudik (homecoming travel) deals

**Phase 4: Pre-Lebaran (Final week)**
- Peak spending: THR received, gift buying frenzy
- Flash sales, last-minute deals
- "Diskon Lebaran" across all platforms
- 87% of Indonesians actively shop during this period

**Phase 5: Lebaran & Post-Lebaran**
- "Selamat Hari Raya" greetings across all channels
- Post-Lebaran sales (clearance)
- Back-to-routine content

**Ad budget tip:** Increase spend 50-100% during Ramadan month.
CPM rises, but conversion rates also spike significantly.

## Local Payment Optimization

### Payment Methods by Preference
1. **Bank Transfer** (still most popular for larger purchases)
2. **E-wallets:** GoPay, OVO, Dana, ShopeePay, LinkAja
3. **QRIS** (universal QR payment standard — works across all wallets)
4. **COD** (Cash on Delivery — critical for marketplace sellers)
5. **Virtual Account** (BCA, BNI, BRI, Mandiri)
6. **Installments:** Kredivo, Akulaku, Home Credit, Atome
7. **Credit card** (lower penetration, urban demographics)

### Payment Integration Tips
- Support minimum 3 payment methods (e-wallet, bank, COD)
- Display QRIS for physical touchpoints
- Highlight installment options prominently ("Cicilan 0% 12 bulan")
- Show "Gratis Ongkir" (free shipping) badge — #1 conversion driver
- Include COD option for marketplace — increases reach to non-banked consumers
