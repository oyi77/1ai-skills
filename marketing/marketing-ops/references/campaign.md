```markdown

## Channel Plan
| Channel | Role | Budget | Content Needed | KPI |

|---------|----------|----------|---------------|
| {Headline} | {Version A} | {Version B} | {CTR} |

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| {Risk} | {H/M/L} | {H/M/L} | {Plan} |
```

## Launch Playbook

### Product Launch Timeline (Reverse-Engineered)

**8 Weeks Before Launch**
- [ ] Define launch objectives, success metrics, and budget
- [ ] Finalize messaging and positioning
- [ ] Brief creative team / agency
- [ ] Set up tracking and attribution
- [ ] Begin building anticipation content

**6 Weeks Before**
- [ ] Create all content assets (blog, email, social, ads, landing page)
- [ ] Build or update landing page
- [ ] Set up email sequences (pre-launch, launch, post-launch)
- [ ] Begin influencer/partner outreach

**4 Weeks Before**
- [ ] Finalize ad creative and audiences
- [ ] QA all landing pages, forms, tracking
- [ ] Schedule social content
- [ ] Press/PR outreach if applicable
- [ ] Internal team briefing

**2 Weeks Before**
- [ ] Teaser campaign begins (social, email)
- [ ] Warm audiences primed (retargeting pools ready)
- [ ] All assets reviewed and approved
- [ ] Backup plan documented (if launch delays)

**1 Week Before**
- [ ] Final QA — every link, form, pixel, UTM tested
- [ ] Email sequences armed and scheduled
- [ ] Ad campaigns set to launch (paused, ready)
- [ ] Team communication plan confirmed

**Launch Day**
- [ ] Activate all campaigns simultaneously
- [ ] Send launch email to full list
- [ ] Post across all social channels
- [ ] Monitor in real-time: dashboards, support inbox, social mentions
- [ ] Respond to early engagement quickly

**Week 1 Post-Launch**
- [ ] Daily performance monitoring
- [ ] Optimize ads based on early data
- [ ] Send social proof email (early results, testimonials)
- [ ] Address FAQ/objections via content

**Week 2-4 Post-Launch**
- [ ] Weekly optimization of ad spend and creative
- [ ] A/B test refinements
- [ ] Retargeting warm audiences
- [ ] Case study / testimonial collection
- [ ] Post-launch report and retrospective

## Budget Allocation

### Campaign Budget Template

```markdown
## Campaign Budget: {Campaign Name}
**Total Budget:** ${total}

### Channel Allocation
| Channel | % of Budget | Amount | Expected Result |
|---------|------------|--------|----------------|
| Paid Social (Meta) | {%} | ${X} | {leads/sales} |
| Paid Search (Google) | {%} | ${X} | {leads/sales} |
| Email Marketing | {%} | ${X} | {leads/sales} |
| Content Production | {%} | ${X} | {assets created} |
| Influencer/PR | {%} | ${X} | {reach/mentions} |
| Creative Production | {%} | ${X} | {assets delivered} |
| Tools & Software | {%} | ${X} | {platform costs} |
| Contingency | 10% | ${X} | {buffer} |
| **TOTAL** | **100%** | **${total}** | |

### Budget Rules
- Reserve 10% as contingency (reallocate to best performers mid-campaign)
- First 20% of budget is "learning spend" — don't judge ROAS until past this
- Reallocate weekly from underperforming to overperforming channels
- Track actual vs planned weekly
```
