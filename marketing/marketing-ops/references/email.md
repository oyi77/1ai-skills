## Email Sequence Types

| Sequence | Trigger | Length | Goal |
|----------|---------|--------|------|
| Welcome | New signup | 5-7 emails over 14 days | Onboard, activate, build trust |
| Nurture | Lead magnet download | 4-6 emails over 21 days | Educate, move toward purchase |
| Launch | Product release | 5-8 emails over 7-10 days | Drive purchases, create urgency |
| Win-back | Inactive 30-90 days | 3-4 emails over 14 days | Re-engage or clean list |
| Cart abandon | Abandoned cart | 3 emails over 3 days | Recover the sale |
| Post-purchase | After purchase | 3-5 emails over 30 days | Onboard, upsell, get review |
| Referral | Active customer | 2-3 emails | Generate referrals |

## Welcome Sequence

### 5-Email Welcome Template

**Email 1 — Instant (Within 5 minutes)**
```
Subject: Welcome to {Brand} — here's what's next
Purpose: Deliver the promised lead magnet/access, set expectations
Content:
- Thank them for joining
- Deliver what was promised (download link, access instructions)
- Tell them what to expect (email frequency, content type)
- One quick win they can achieve right now
CTA: Use the product / read the guide / take first action
```

**Email 2 — Day 2**
```
Subject: The #1 mistake {audience} make with {topic}
Purpose: Provide immediate value, establish authority
Content:
- Address a common pain point
- Share a quick insight or framework
- Position your solution subtly
CTA: Read the full guide / Watch the video
```

**Email 3 — Day 4**
```
Subject: How {customer name} achieved {specific result}
Purpose: Social proof, build desire
Content:
- Customer story / case study
- Specific numbers and outcomes
- What they did differently
CTA: See how it works / Start your trial
```

**Email 4 — Day 7**
```
Subject: {First name}, are you stuck on {common obstacle}?
Purpose: Overcome objections, provide deeper education
Content:
- Address common hesitations
- FAQ-style answers
- Additional resources or training
CTA: Reply with questions / Book a demo
```

**Email 5 — Day 10-14**
```
Subject: This won't last — {specific offer/deadline}
Purpose: Convert to next step
Content:
- Summarize the value they've received
- Present the offer clearly
- Add urgency (limited time, limited spots) — only if genuine
CTA: Upgrade / Purchase / Sign up
```

## Nurture Sequence

### Content Arc Structure
1. **Problem awareness** — "Did you know this is costing you...?"
2. **Solution education** — "Here's how leading companies solve this..."
3. **Product connection** — "This is exactly why we built..."
4. **Social proof** — "See what {customer} achieved..."
5. **Soft CTA** — "When you're ready, here's how to start..."
6. **Direct CTA** — "Special offer for you..."

### Segmentation Triggers
Track engagement to segment subscribers:
- **Engaged** (opens + clicks within 7 days) → Move to sales sequence
- **Interested** (opens but no clicks) → Continue nurture
- **Passive** (no opens in 14 days) → Re-engagement sequence
- **Dead** (no opens in 90 days) → Sunset / clean

### 7-Day Launch Email Plan

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| -7 | Teaser | Build anticipation: "Something new is coming..." |
| -3 | Sneak peek | Show behind-the-scenes, early details |

| +1 | Social proof | First results, testimonials, social buzz |

| +5 | Last chance | Final reminder with scarcity (if applicable) |

## Re-engagement Sequence

### 3-Email Win-back Template

**Email 1 — "We miss you"**
```
Subject: It's been a while, {name}...
Tone: Warm, no pressure
Content: Remind them why they signed up. Share what's new.
CTA: Come check out what's changed
```

**Email 2 — "Here's what you're missing" (Day 5)**
```
Subject: You missed this → {most popular recent content}
Content: Curate 2-3 best pieces of content since they went quiet
CTA: Catch up on the best stuff
```

**Email 3 — "Stay or go?" (Day 10)**
```
Subject: Should I stop emailing you?
Content: Give them a clear choice: stay on list or unsubscribe
CTA: "Yes, keep me" button → stays / No action → auto-remove in 7 days
```

## Subject Line Optimization

### Proven Subject Line Formulas
- **Curiosity gap:** "The {topic} trick nobody talks about"
- **How-to:** "How to {achieve result} without {common pain}"
- **Number:** "{Number} ways to {benefit} (#{X} is surprising)"
- **Question:** "Are you making this {topic} mistake?"
- **Personal:** "{Name}, your {topic} report is ready"
- **Urgency:** "Last chance: {offer} ends tonight"
- **Social proof:** "Why {X} marketers switched to {product}"

### Subject Line Rules
- 30-50 characters for mobile optimization
- Avoid ALL CAPS and excessive punctuation (!!!!)
- Avoid spam triggers: "free," "act now," "guaranteed," "no obligation"
- Use preview text as extension, not repetition
- Test emoji — works for some brands, not others
- Always write 3-5 variants for A/B testing

## Email Copywriting Rules

### The 5 Email Copy Principles
1. **One email = one idea.** Don't overload with multiple topics.
2. **Write like you talk.** Conversational, not corporate.
3. **Short paragraphs.** 1-3 sentences max. Use white space.
4. **One clear CTA.** Repeat it, but it should always be the same action.
5. **Value first, ask second.** Give before you take.

### Formatting for Readability
- Paragraphs: 1-3 sentences
- Sentence length: under 20 words ideal
- Line width: under 600px (mobile-friendly)
- Bold key phrases sparingly
- Use bullet points for lists
- CTA buttons > text links (higher CTR)

## Deliverability & Health

### Pre-Send Checklist
- [ ] SPF, DKIM, DMARC records configured
- [ ] Sending from authenticated domain (not free email)
- [ ] List cleaned in last 90 days (remove bounces, complaints)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link prominent and working
- [ ] Physical address in footer (CAN-SPAM compliance)
- [ ] Tested in multiple email clients (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail)
- [ ] Images have alt text
- [ ] Links all working and tracked

### Key Health Metrics

| Metric | Healthy | Warning | Critical |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| Open rate | > 25% | 15-25% | < 15% |
| Click rate | > 3% | 1-3% | < 1% |
| Bounce rate | < 1% | 1-3% | > 3% |
| Unsubscribe rate | < 0.3% | 0.3-0.5% | > 0.5% |
| Spam complaint rate | < 0.05% | 0.05-0.1% | > 0.1% |

### List Hygiene Schedule
- **Weekly:** Monitor bounce rate, remove hard bounces
- **Monthly:** Clean soft bounces (3+ consecutive), check spam traps
- **Quarterly:** Re-engagement campaign for 90-day inactives
- **Bi-annually:** Full list verification with email validation service
