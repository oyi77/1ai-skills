## Influencer Tiers & Selection

### Tier Definitions
| Tier | Followers | Typical Cost | Best For |
|------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | $50-500 / product gifting | Local reach, niche credibility, high engagement |
| Micro | 10K-100K | $500-5,000 | Targeted niches, authentic endorsement |
| Mid-tier | 100K-500K | $5,000-25,000 | Balanced reach + engagement |
| Macro | 500K-1M | $25,000-100,000 | Mass awareness, brand campaigns |
| Mega / Celebrity | 1M+ | $100,000+ | Maximum reach, brand association |

### Selection Criteria (Score 1-10 Each)
1. **Audience fit** — Does their audience match your buyer persona?
2. **Engagement rate** — Real engagement, not bought followers
3. **Content quality** — Would you be proud to be associated?
4. **Brand alignment** — Do their values match yours?
5. **Authenticity** — Do they only promote what they genuinely use?
6. **Previous partnerships** — Track record with other brands?
7. **Platform match** — Strong on your priority platform?

### Red Flags (Walk Away)
- Follower-to-engagement ratio way off (100K followers, 50 likes)
- Only promotional content (no organic, personal posts)
- Promotes competing products frequently
- History of controversy that conflicts with your brand
- Won't disclose sponsored content properly
- Demands full payment upfront with no deliverable commitment

### Vetting Checklist
- [ ] Manual scroll through last 30 posts — quality and consistency
- [ ] Check comments — real conversations or bot spam?
- [ ] Review audience demographics (many tools show this)
- [ ] Search their name + "controversy" or "scandal"
- [ ] Check other brands they've worked with
- [ ] Ask for a media kit with audience demographics and case studies

## Campaign Types

### Product Seeding (Gifting)
- Send product free, no obligation to post
- Low cost, authentic if they genuinely like it
- Works best with nano/micro influencers
- Include a personal note, not a generic PR package

### Sponsored Posts
- Paid content creation featuring your product
- Specify: number of posts, platforms, content format, timeline
- Always require FTC/local disclosure ("Ad", "#sponsored", "#paid")
- Brief them but don't script — authenticity matters more than perfection

### Affiliate / Commission
- Influencer earns commission per sale/signup
- Lower risk for brand (pay for performance)
- Unique discount code or tracked link per influencer
- Typical commission: 15-30% for digital, 5-15% for physical

### Brand Ambassador
- Ongoing relationship (3-12 months)
- Regular content cadence (e.g., 2 posts/month)
- Exclusive to your category (they don't promote competitors)
- Deeper integration: beta access, feedback, co-creation

### UGC Creator
- Create content for YOUR channels (not theirs)
- They make it, you own it and distribute it
- Great for ad creative — UGC-style ads outperform branded
- Typically $150-500 per video for UGC creators

### Co-Creation
- Develop a product, feature, or content series together
- Limited edition product with influencer's name
- Joint webinar, podcast, or live stream
- Highest effort, highest authenticity

## Outreach & Negotiation

### Outreach Message Template
```
Subject: Collab idea — {Brand} × {Creator Name}

Hi {Name},

I've been following your content about {specific topic they cover} —
especially {specific post/video you genuinely liked}.

I'm {your name} from {brand}. We {what you do in one sentence}.

I think there's a natural fit between your audience and what we're building.
Would you be open to a quick chat about a potential collaboration?

No pressure either way — I genuinely enjoy your content regardless.

Best,
{Your name}
{Your title, brand}
```

### Negotiation Framework
- **Start with value exchange,** not payment
- Ask for their rate card first — don't anchor low
- Negotiate on deliverables, not just price
- Offer creative freedom (their audience trusts them, not your script)
- Include usage rights in the agreement (can you use their content in ads?)
- Consider performance bonuses tied to results

### Contract Must-Haves
- Deliverables: number, format, platforms, timeline
- Approval process: how many revision rounds
- Content ownership and usage rights
- Disclosure requirements (FTC, local regulations)
- Payment terms and schedule
- Exclusivity period (if any)
- Performance metrics and reporting
- Cancellation terms

## UGC Strategy

### Generating Organic UGC
- Create a branded hashtag and promote it
- Run contests ("share your story with {product}")
- Feature UGC on your official channels (with credit)
- Make the product "Instagrammable" — worth sharing
- Ask for reviews systematically post-purchase
- Create share-worthy packaging or unboxing experience

### UGC for Ads
UGC-style ad creative outperforms branded content for most DTC brands:
- Authentic, phone-filmed quality (not overproduced)
- Real person talking directly to camera
- Problem → discovery → result story arc
- Include specific results or genuine reactions
- Add captions (85% watch without sound)

### UGC Sourcing
1. Customer reviews → turn best quotes into graphics
2. Social mentions → reshare with permission
3. UGC creator platforms → hire creators to make ad-style content
4. Employee advocacy → team members create behind-the-scenes
5. Community challenges → themed content creation events

## Measurement & ROI

### Influencer Campaign Metrics
| Metric | How to Measure | Benchmark |
|--------|---------------|-----------|
| Reach | Total impressions across all posts | Varies by tier |
| Engagement rate | (Likes+Comments+Shares) ÷ Reach | >3% good, >5% excellent |
| Click-throughs | UTM tracked links per influencer | Track per creator |
| Conversions | Unique discount codes or tracked links | Track per creator |
| Cost per engagement | Total cost ÷ Total engagements | <$0.50 for micro |
| Cost per acquisition | Total cost ÷ Conversions | Varies by product |
| EMV (Earned Media Value) | Equivalent ad spend for same reach | 3-5x cost = good ROI |
| Brand lift | Surveys, search volume, social mentions | Pre/post campaign |

### Attribution Methods
- **Unique discount codes:** Each influencer gets a unique code
- **UTM links:** Trackable links per influencer
- **Landing pages:** Dedicated page per influencer or campaign
- **Post-purchase survey:** "How did you hear about us?"
- **Promo code + link combined:** Belt-and-suspenders approach

## Ambassador Programs

### Program Structure
```
Tier 1 — Fans (Entry)
  Requirements: Follow + engage regularly
  Benefits: Early access, community badge, swag
  
Tier 2 — Creators (Active)
  Requirements: Create content monthly, use affiliate link
  Benefits: Commission (15-25%), free products, features on brand page
  
Tier 3 — Partners (VIP)
  Requirements: Consistent performance, exclusive to brand
  Benefits: Higher commission (25-40%), co-creation, event invites, advisory role
```

### Scaling Ambassador Programs
- Start with 10-20 ambassadors, prove the model
- Build a simple application form (Google Form works)
- Create a shared resource hub: brand assets, product info, content guidelines
- Monthly newsletter to ambassadors: product updates, content ideas, top performers
- Recognize top performers publicly (leaderboard, features, bonuses)
- Use ambassador management tools at scale (PartnerStack, Dub Partners, Refersion)

## Platform-Specific Tactics

### Instagram
- Reels collaborations (double the audience exposure)
- Story takeovers (influencer "runs" your account for a day)
- Carousel co-creation (educational content with both brands)
- Live shopping events

### TikTok
- Trend-based challenges with branded hashtag
- Duets and stitches with brand content
- "Get Ready With Me" featuring product
- Behind-the-scenes / day-in-the-life integrations

### YouTube
- Dedicated review or tutorial videos
- Integration within existing content (30-60 second segment)
- Sponsored series (multi-episode collaboration)
- Shorts for quick product highlights

### LinkedIn (B2B)
- Thought leadership posts mentioning/featuring your tool
- Carousel case studies created by the influencer
- LinkedIn Live co-hosted sessions
- Newsletter features and cross-promotions

### WhatsApp / Messaging (Indonesia & LatAm)
- Influencer shares product link in their Status
- Broadcast list recommendations from trusted community leaders
- Group admin endorsements in relevant WhatsApp groups
