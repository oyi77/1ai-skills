# Twitter/X Automation Skill

## Overview

Automate your Twitter/X presence with AI-powered tools for content posting, engagement automation, and audience growth. This skill covers posting automation, reply handling, follower management, and analytics tracking.

**Key Platforms**: X (Twitter), Typefully, Postwise, Hypefury, OpenTweet  
**Best For**: Solopreneurs, indie hackers, SaaS founders, content creators

---

## When to Use

- Schedule and automate tweet posting
- Auto-engage with followers and mentions
- Build audience on autopilot
- Track engagement analytics
- Run "build in public" campaigns
- Monitor competitors and trends
- Generate content from RSS feeds, GitHub, Stripe

---

## When NOT to Use

- Genuine personal engagement (automated responses feel robotic)
- Crisis management (human oversight required)
- Platform policy violations (avoid spam behavior)
- Building deep relationships (use for initial contact only)

## Best Practices

### Do's
✅ Post consistently (5-15 tweets/day for growth)  
✅ Use threads for long-form content  
✅ Engage within first hour of posting  
✅ A/B test posting times  
✅ Repurpose content from other platforms  

### Don'ts
❌ Don't over-automate (feels robotic)  
❌ Don't buy followers  
❌ Don't spam hashtags  
❌ Don't ignore negative feedback  
❌ Don't automate replies without review
